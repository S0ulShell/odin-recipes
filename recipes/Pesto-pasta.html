Pesto Pasta

Description

Basic pesto pasta that is as green as a college student after a 3 day bender

Ingredients

  • Tagliatelle pasta, 250g
  • Green pesto, 1dl
  • Mozzarella, 125g
  • Cherry tomatoes, 250g
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta according to the instructions on the package. Drain the water and mix in the pesto.
  2. Cut the tomatoes in half, cut the the mozzarella. Mix them with the pasta.
  3. Add fresh basil, serve fresh!
  4. Enjoy :)
